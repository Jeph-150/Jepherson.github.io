Our Classes

Salsa

Get ready to move and groove with our vibrant salsa classes! Learn the sultry steps and rhythmic patterns of this iconic Latin dance, perfect for couples or solo dancers. Whether you're a beginner or looking to refine your skills, our expert instructors will have you salsa-ing with style and confidence in no time!

Bachata

Indulge in the romantic allure of bachata with our captivating classes! Discover the sensual movements and intimate connection of this Dominican dance form. From basic steps to intricate footwork, our skilled instructors will guide you through every beat, ensuring you feel the passion and rhythm of bachata with every step.

Personal lessons: $50/h

Merengue

Shake things up with the lively and energetic beats of merengue! Join our dynamic classes to learn the spirited movements and infectious rhythms of this Dominican dance sensation. Whether you're a beginner or seasoned dancer, our enthusiastic instructors will have you twirling and swaying to the vibrant melodies of merengue in no time!

Personal lessons: $50/h

Kompa

Embark on a rhythmic journey with our exhilarating kompa classes! Dive into the infectious beats and lively movements of this Haitian dance style, blending elements of African and European influences. Our experienced instructors will teach you the essential steps and techniques, allowing you to immerse yourself in the joyous spirit of kompa while exploring its rich cultural heritage.

Personal lessons: $50/h
