Who are we

At Let's Dance, we believe in the power of dance to bring joy, fitness, and community to people's lives. Founded in 2020, our studio offers a welcoming space for dancers of all ages and skill levels to learn, grow, and express themselves through movement.

We offer a wide range of classes in Salsa, Bachata, Meringue, Kompa, and more, taught by our team of experienced and passionate instructors. Whether you're a beginner looking to learn the basics or an experienced dancer aiming to refine your skills, we have the right class for you.

Join us for group classes, private lessons, or our exciting social dance nights where you can practice your moves, meet new friends, and have a great time. At Let's Dance, everyone is welcome, and we look forward to dancing with you!
